DEFQON 1 2022


DAY 1

Day 1 of the festival started with many artists lined-up for a spectacular performance at different stages across the festival venue
With the massive mainstage design the fans anf the attendees of the festival were in for a treat.
Due to the pandemic the festival never took place for 2 years, but now it is back in 2022 with a bang.
With insane production of the stage with lasers, Pyros & strobes it was surely to light up the sky when darkness fall.
Day 1 came to an end with a segment they call Spotlight. This year SUB-ZERO PROJECT were the ones closing the day 1 and the spotlight.


DAY 2

Day 2 was even more power packed as this was the day of POWER-HOUR
Power-Hour is been going on since the day this festival started. this initiative brings a twist to the modern exercise times.
All the attendees gather at the mainstage and with the help of fitness trainers all of them have a synchronised workout session.
Of course nothing is complete at defqon without Hardstyle Music.
A twist of hardcore workout with Hardstyle Music, this was Power-hour on day 2.
The day was also lined up with a set of new and emerging artists in the scene as well as some defqon legends.
Day 2 comes to an end with the Ever awaited ENDSHOW. One of the highlights of the festival where Music is united with Fireworks and lights causing the most amazing show person could ever see in his life.
